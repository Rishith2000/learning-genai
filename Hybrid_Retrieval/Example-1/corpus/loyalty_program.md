# Loyalty Program Handbook

## Program Overview
The rewards program is free to join and requires only an email address and phone
number. Members earn 1 point for every $1 spent before tax. Points post to the
account within 24 hours of the transaction settling.

## Redemption Rate
Every 500 points converts to a $5 reward certificate. Certificates are issued
automatically at the close of the earning cycle and expire 90 days after issue.
Points themselves expire after 18 months of account inactivity.

## Membership Tiers
There are three tiers based on trailing twelve month spend. Bronze is the entry
tier for members spending under $500. Silver applies from $500 to $1,499 and adds
a 5 percent birthday discount. Gold applies at $1,500 and above and includes free
standard shipping, early access to sales, and a dedicated support line.

## Tier Evaluation
Tiers are recalculated on the first day of each month based on the trailing twelve
months. A member who drops below a threshold keeps their tier for a 60 day grace
period before downgrading.

## Missing Points
If points do not appear after 24 hours, the associate submits a points adjustment
through the LOY-3300 form with the receipt barcode. Adjustments are approved within
two business days. Points cannot be granted for purchases older than 60 days.

## Stacking Rules
Reward certificates can be combined with sale prices and with the employee discount
but cannot be combined with a price match adjustment on the same line item.
