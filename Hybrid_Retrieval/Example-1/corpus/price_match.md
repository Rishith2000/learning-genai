# Price Match and Price Adjustment

## Competitor Price Match
We match the advertised price of any local competitor with a physical storefront
within 25 miles, and of the following online retailers only: major national
electronics and general merchandise sites listed in the approved competitor list.
The competitor item must be identical in brand, model number, color, and size, and
must be currently in stock at the competitor.

## Exclusions
We do not match marketplace or third party seller listings, auction sites, prices
requiring a membership or subscription to see, doorbuster or limited quantity
promotions, clearance or open box pricing, typographical errors, or prices from
sellers that do not have physical inventory. Bundle pricing is not matched against
single item pricing.

## Price Adjustment After Purchase
If our own price drops within 14 days of the customer's purchase, the customer is
entitled to a one time adjustment refunding the difference to the original payment
method. The customer must present the receipt or the order number. Adjustments are
not available on clearance markdowns or on items purchased with a price match.

## Approval Thresholds
Associates may approve a price match of up to $50 difference without escalation.
Differences between $50 and $200 require a supervisor override using the PM-0090
override code. Anything above $200 requires store manager approval and is logged
for review.

## Documentation
The associate must capture evidence of the competitor price, either a screenshot
with a visible date or the printed circular, and attach it to the transaction. Price
matches without attached evidence are reversed during weekly audit.
