# Employee Policies

## Employee Discount
Active associates receive 25 percent off regular priced merchandise and 10 percent
off already discounted merchandise. The discount is applied with code EMP25 at the
register and requires the associate to scan their badge. The discount extends to a
spouse or domestic partner and to dependent children listed in the HR system.

## Discount Exclusions
The employee discount does not apply to gift cards, licensed lottery products,
prepaid wireless, or major appliances already on promotional pricing. It cannot be
combined with a price match on the same item. Abuse of the discount, including
purchasing for resale or for non household members, is grounds for termination.

## Scheduling and Time Off
Schedules post 14 days in advance in the workforce system. Shift swaps require both
associates to accept in the app and a manager to approve. Requests for time off must
be submitted at least 21 days ahead. Blackout periods apply from the Friday before
Thanksgiving through December 26 and no discretionary time off is approved during
that window.

## Meal and Rest Breaks
Associates working more than six hours receive an unpaid 30 minute meal break and
two paid 15 minute rest breaks. Breaks must be clocked. Working through a meal break
requires manager approval and is recorded as an exception.

## Dress Code
Associates wear the issued polo or button down with the name badge visible, closed
toe shoes, and no visible offensive graphics. Denim without holes is acceptable.
Safety shoes are required for anyone working in receiving or operating equipment.
