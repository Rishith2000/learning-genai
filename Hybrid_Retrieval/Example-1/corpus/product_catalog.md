# Product Catalog Reference

## NX-4400 Espresso Machine
The NX-4400 is a 15 bar pump espresso machine with a 1.8 liter removable water
tank, a built in conical burr grinder, and a steam wand for milk texturing. It
draws 1350 watts and measures 12 by 14 by 15 inches. Retail price is $449.99. The
NX-4400 ships with a portafilter, two baskets, and a tamper. Descaling is
recommended every 90 days.

## NX-2200 Drip Coffee Maker
The NX-2200 is a 12 cup programmable drip brewer with a thermal carafe and a 24
hour delay start. Retail price is $89.99. It uses a size 4 cone filter. The warming
plate is disabled when the thermal carafe is installed.

## VT-880 Cordless Vacuum
The VT-880 cordless stick vacuum runs 45 minutes on a full charge and recharges in
3.5 hours. It weighs 6.2 pounds and includes a crevice tool and a motorized pet
brush. Retail price is $329.99. The dust bin capacity is 0.7 liters. Replacement
filters are sold under SKU VT-880-FLT.

## AB-1500 Air Purifier
The AB-1500 covers rooms up to 500 square feet and uses a three stage filter with a
true HEPA layer. Retail price is $199.99. The filter indicator resets by holding the
mode button for five seconds. Replacement filter SKU is AB-1500-HEPA and should be
changed every six months.

## Compatibility Notes
The VT-880 battery is not compatible with the older VT-660. The NX-4400 portafilter
fits the NX-4600 but not the NX-2200, which is not an espresso machine. Accessories
are listed on the product page under the Compatible Items tab.
