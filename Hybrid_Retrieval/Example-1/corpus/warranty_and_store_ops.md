# Warranty and Store Operations

## Manufacturer Warranty
Most electronics and small appliances carry a one year limited manufacturer warranty
covering defects in materials and workmanship. The warranty does not cover accidental
damage, liquid damage, cosmetic wear, or damage from using the product outside its
stated purpose. Proof of purchase is required for any warranty claim.

## Extended Protection Plan
The extended protection plan adds two or three additional years of coverage beyond
the manufacturer warranty and includes accidental damage from handling. The plan must
be purchased within 30 days of the item. Plan claims are filed through the provider
portal, not through the store. A customer who wants to cancel the plan receives a
prorated refund within the first 60 days.

## Defective Merchandise Within 30 Days
An item that fails within the first 30 days is treated as a standard return and is
exchanged or refunded in store. Do not send the customer to the manufacturer during
the first 30 days. After 30 days, direct the customer to the manufacturer warranty or
the protection plan, whichever applies.

## Store Hours
Stores open at 9:00 AM and close at 9:00 PM Monday through Saturday, and open 10:00 AM
to 7:00 PM on Sunday. Stores close at 6:00 PM on Christmas Eve and are closed entirely
on Thanksgiving Day, Christmas Day, and Easter Sunday.

## Closing Procedure
At close, the manager on duty counts the safe, runs the end of day report, verifies the
alarm is armed, and confirms all associates have clocked out. Cash over or short by more
than $20 requires an incident report the same night.

## Inventory Cycle Counts
Cycle counts run weekly for high shrink categories and monthly for everything else.
Variances above 2 percent of on hand value trigger a recount before any adjustment is
posted to the inventory system.
