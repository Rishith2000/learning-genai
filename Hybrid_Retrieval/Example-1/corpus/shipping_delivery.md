# Shipping and Delivery

## Free Shipping Threshold
Standard ground shipping is free on orders totaling $75 or more after discounts
and before tax. Orders below the threshold are charged a flat $6.95. Loyalty
members at the Gold tier receive free standard shipping on every order with no
minimum.

## Delivery Speeds
Standard ground delivers in 3 to 6 business days. Expedited two-day shipping is
$14.95. Overnight shipping is $29.95 and is not available to PO boxes, Alaska,
Hawaii, or US territories.

## Order Cutoff Time
Orders placed before 2:00 PM Eastern on a business day ship the same day. Orders
placed after the cutoff, on weekends, or on a company holiday ship the next
business day. The cutoff is enforced by the warehouse management system and cannot
be overridden by store associates.

## Ship From Store
When a store fulfills an online order, the associate picks the item, scans it into
the SFS-220 handoff queue, and stages it for carrier pickup. Carrier pickup occurs
daily at 4:00 PM. Items not staged by 3:45 PM roll to the next day.

## Damaged in Transit
If a package arrives damaged, the customer files a claim within 14 days of
delivery. The associate opens a transit claim, uploads photos, and issues an
immediate replacement without waiting for carrier resolution. Do not ask the
customer to return a transit damaged item.

## Address Changes
Shipping addresses can be changed only while the order is in Pending status. Once
the order moves to Picked, the address is locked and the order must be cancelled
and re-placed.
