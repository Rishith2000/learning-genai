# Returns and Exchanges Policy

## Standard Return Window
Customers may return most merchandise within 30 days of the purchase date for a
full refund to the original payment method. A valid receipt or the order number
from the customer's account is required. Items returned between day 31 and day 60
are eligible for store credit only, issued at the current selling price.

## Condition Requirements
Returned merchandise must be unused, in its original packaging, and include all
manuals, cables, and accessories that shipped with it. Associates should inspect
for missing components before accepting a return. Merchandise showing wear beyond
normal inspection is refused and logged under reason code RTN-DAMAGE.

## Final Sale Categories
The following categories are final sale and cannot be returned: clearance items
marked with a yellow tag, opened consumables, gift cards, and personalized or
engraved goods. Seasonal decor purchased after December 20 is also final sale.

## Restocking Fees
Large appliances and furniture over $500 carry a 15 percent restocking fee unless
the item is defective. Defective merchandise never incurs a restocking fee.

## RMA Process for Online Orders
Online returns require a Return Merchandise Authorization. The associate opens
the RMA-4471 workflow in the order management system, selects the reason code,
and prints the prepaid label. The RMA number must be written on the outside of
the carton. Refunds post 3 to 5 business days after the warehouse scans the return.

## Receipt Lookup
If the customer has no receipt, associates can look up the transaction with the
last four digits of the payment card within the 90 day lookup window. Cash
purchases without a receipt are refunded as store credit at the lowest sale price
of the prior 30 days.
